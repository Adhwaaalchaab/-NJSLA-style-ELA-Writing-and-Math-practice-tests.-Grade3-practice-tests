NJSLA Grade 3 Writing Rubric Reference
Organization (4 pts)

4 — Effective: clear intro, body, conclusion; transitions

3 — Adequate: structure present; some transitions

2 — Inconsistent: weak intro or conclusion; few transitions

1 — Minimal: no clear structure; hard to follow

Evidence Use (4 pts)

4 — Thorough: both sources used; accurate; clearly cited

3 — Adequate: both sources; mostly accurate

2 — Partial: may use one source; limited detail

1 — Minimal: little or no source evidence

Development (4 pts)

4 — Full elaboration; domain vocabulary; deep understanding

3 — Some elaboration; some vocabulary; shows understanding

2 — Limited elaboration; basic vocabulary

1 — Ideas not developed or explained

Grammar & Conventions (2 pts)

2 — Few or no errors; demonstrates Grade 3 writing control

1 — Some errors; meaning is mostly clear

0 — Many errors that interfere with meaning
