Open Response Scoring Guide (NJSLA)
3 Points — Full Credit
Correct answer with complete work shown. Reasoning is clear and mathematically sound. No errors.
2 Points — Partial Credit
Shows correct approach with minor errors, OR correct answer with incomplete explanation, OR one part of a two-part problem correct.
1 Point — Minimal Credit
Shows some mathematical understanding. Partial work shown. Significant errors present but approach is not completely wrong.
