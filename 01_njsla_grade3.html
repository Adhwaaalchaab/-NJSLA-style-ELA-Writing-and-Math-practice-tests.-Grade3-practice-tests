Writing Task

You have read two sources about animals that help people and nature. Now you will write an informational article to share what you learned.

Your Writing Prompt

Write an informational article explaining how animals help people and the world around them. Use important details from BOTH sources to support your ideas.

Remember to:
  • Introduce your topic clearly
  • Use facts and details from both sources
  • Organize your writing so it is easy to follow
  • Use your own words
  • Write a conclusion that wraps up your ideas
  • Check your spelling, punctuation, and grammar

NJSLA Grade 3 Rubric Reference
Organization/Purpose (4 pts)

4 — Effective: clear intro, body, conclusion; transitions used

3 — Adequate: intro/conclusion present; some transitions

2 — Inconsistent: weak intro or conclusion; few transitions

1 — Minimal: no clear structure; hard to follow

Evidence/Elaboration (4 pts)

4 — Thorough: both sources; accurate; elaborated; vocabulary

3 — Adequate: both sources; mostly accurate; some elaboration

2 — Partial: may use one source; limited elaboration

1 — Minimal: little evidence; inaccurate; no elaboration

Conventions (2 pts)

2 — Few/no errors; grade-level control

1 — Some errors; meaning still clear

0 — Many errors; meaning is unclear
